1. Privacy Policy

  • We collect basic user information such as name, email, book listings, and activity logs.
  • Your data is used for account management, book exchange coordination, and improving the platform.
  • We do not sell personal data; sharing occurs only with consent or legal requirements.
  • We use reasonable security measures, but no system is 100% safe.
  • Users may request to update or delete their data at any time.

2. Community Guidelines

  • Users must behave respectfully, avoiding harassment or inappropriate content.
  • All book listings should be accurate and truthful.
  • Users are responsible for safe exchange arrangements and checking books before completing a swap.

3. Prohibited Activities

  • Listing stolen, illegal, or copyrighted books without permission.
  • Spamming, phishing, hacking attempts, or sharing harmful content.
  • Creating fake accounts or misleading other users.

4. Content Ownership

  • Users own their uploaded content, including book photos and descriptions.
  • By posting, you grant us permission to display your content on the platform.

5. Cookies Policy

  • We use cookies to maintain login sessions and enhance user experience.
  • Cookies help personalize book suggestions and improve performance.
  • You may disable cookies, but some features may not work properly.

6. Updates to Policies

  • We may update these policies at any time.
  • Changes become effective upon posting.
  • Your continued use means you accept the updated policies.
